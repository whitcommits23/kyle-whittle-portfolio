Case Studies

What it looks like in practice.

Canopy Challenge

No structured onboarding ecosystem. New customers were left to figure out the product on their own, leading to early churn and overwhelmed support teams.

Approach

Designed and launched a connected training ecosystem: Knowledge Base, LMS, video library, and two certification programs, all built around a structured customer learning path.

Result

30% onboarding efficiency improvement • 40% churn reduction

Entrata Challenge

Zendesk knowledge base grown to 1,500+ articles across 60+ product lines with no governance, inconsistent taxonomy, and declining self-service rates.

Approach

Directed a full-scale theme migration, introduced AI-powered search, rebuilt article taxonomy, and established a Jira-driven content workflow with clear SLAs.

Result

45% self-service adoption increase • 35% faster content turnaround

Qualtrics Challenge

Instructor-led training couldn't keep pace with customer growth across global markets. They needed an on-demand education model that could.

Approach

Built and launched Experience Basecamp on Skilljar LMS, digitizing 70+ training courses and creating a certification program customers could complete on demand.

Result

$1.6MM revenue generated • 100,000+ article views • 4,000+ likes
