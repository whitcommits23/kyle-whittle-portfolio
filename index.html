What It Looks Like in Practice

CHALLENGE

Canopy had no structured onboarding ecosystem — new customers were left to figure out the product on their own, leading to early churn and overwhelmed support teams.

APPROACH

Designed and launched an integrated training ecosystem: Knowledge Base, LMS, video library, and two certification programs, all built around a structured customer learning path.

RESULT

30% onboarding efficiency improvement • 40% churn reduction

CHALLENGE

Entrata's Zendesk knowledge base had grown to 1,500+ articles across 60+ product lines with no governance, inconsistent taxonomy, and declining self-service rates.

APPROACH

Directed a full-scale theme migration (Copenhagen to Lotus), introduced AI-powered search, rebuilt article taxonomy, and established a Jira-driven content workflow with clear SLAs.

RESULT

45% self-service adoption increase • 35% faster content turnaround

CHALLENGE

Qualtrics needed a scalable customer education revenue stream — instructor-led training wasn't keeping pace with customer growth across global markets.

APPROACH

Built and launched Experience Basecamp on Skilljar LMS, digitizing 70+ training courses and creating a certification program that customers could complete on demand.

RESULT

$1.6MM revenue generated • 100,000+ article views • 4,000+ likes
